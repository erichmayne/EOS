Features

Everything you need to stay accountable

From AI camera tracking to friendly competitions — EOS gives you every tool to build habits that stick.

AI Powered
💪

Live Push-Up Counter

Real-time camera detection counts your reps as you go. Powered by Apple Vision framework — no data leaves your device.

Strava Sync
🏃

Automatic Run Tracking

Connect Strava and your runs count automatically. GPS-verified only — no shortcuts, no manual entries.

Compete
🏆

Fitness Competitions

Challenge friends to push-up or running competitions. Leaderboards, streaks, and bragging rights. Optional entry stakes.

🎯

Custom Goals

Set your own daily targets, deadlines, and schedule. Daily or weekdays. Your rules, your commitment.

🔒

Voluntary Commitment Stakes

Optionally put your own money behind your goals. Complete them and keep every dollar. Your commitment, your choice.

👥

Accountability Partners

Invite friends or family as your designated recipient. Real social accountability with people you know.

How It Works

Four steps to unbreakable habits

1

Set Your Goal

Choose push-ups, running, or both. Set your daily target and deadline.

2

Show Up Daily

Open the app, start your session. AI tracks push-ups. Strava logs runs.

3

Stay Accountable

Optional commitment stakes add real weight to your goals. Compete with friends for extra motivation.

4

Build the Habit

Day by day, rep by rep. Watch your streaks grow and your consistency compound.
