UGC Content Briefs

Creator Video Scripts & Guidelines
The EOS  |  live-eos.com  |  Confidential

Video 1: "Morning Accountability"

30–45 seconds  •  Talking head + B-roll

Concept

Creator talks about how EOS has kept them accountable for early morning runs — the real struggle of getting out of bed and how having stakes on the line changed everything.

Setting & Wardrobe

  • Location: Outdoors — sidewalk, park trail, or running path
  • Outfit: Sporty and put-together — should look just finished or about to start a run
  • Vibe: Energetic, real, relatable. Not overly polished.

Script

Hook (2–3 sec)

"If you have a hard time starting those runs you've been putting off, you have to know this."

Problem (5–8 sec)

"I used to set my alarm for 6 AM every single day... and snooze it every single day. I told myself I'd start running but I had zero accountability."

Solution (10–15 sec)

"Then I found this app called EOS. You set your fitness goals — mine is a 2-mile run every morning — and here's the thing: you put real money on the line. If I don't complete my run before my deadline, my friend gets the money I bet. Once I set it, no backing out."

Proof (5–8 sec)

"It sounds intense but honestly? I haven't missed a morning in three weeks."

CTA (3–5 sec)

"If you need that extra push to actually get out of bed, I'd give this a try. Link's in bio. Trust me, it works."

Video 2: "Winning Money Competing Against Friends"

30–45 seconds  •  Storytelling / talking head

Concept

Creator tells the story of starting a fitness competition with her friends on EOS — the group chat chaos, everyone suddenly becoming a morning person, and the satisfaction of winning the pot. Confident, playful, storytelling energy.

Setting & Wardrobe

  • Location: Outdoors — sidewalk, park trail, or running path
  • Outfit: Sporty and put-together — should look just finished or about to start a run
  • Vibe: Energetic, real, relatable. Not overly polished.

Script

Hook (2–3 sec)

"I just made $500 off my friends and honestly? They should be thanking me."

Setup (5–8 sec)

"OK so me and my girls started this competition on this app called EOS. Everyone threw in money, for whoever hits our goals for running the most days to win the whole thing."

The Fun Part (10–15 sec)

"The group chat became unhinged. Like, girls who have not run since high school were suddenly posting their miles every morning. I was NOT about to lose money AND be the one who quit — so I was locked in. Run done, every single morning."

The Win (5–8 sec)

"Two weeks later the competition ended and the money hit my account. The app tracks your runs through Strava automatically so nobody can fake it — which is important because I don't trust any of them."

CTA (3–5 sec)

"Start one with your friend group on The EOS app. Worst case you get in shape, best case you take their money."

✅ Do's

  • Be expressive — eye rolls, smirks, hand gestures
  • Talk like you're telling your best friend
  • Walk or stretch while talking — keep it moving
  • Say "my girls" / "my friend group" — makes it real
  • Smile at the end — the vibe is fun, not cutthroat

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't over-explain how the app works
  • Don't be actually mean about friends — playful teasing only
  • Don't stand stiff in one spot — move, walk, gesture

Brand Notes

App Name: EOS (pronounced like the word, not spelled out)
Download: App Store → search "The EOS"
Website: live-eos.com
Tone: Confident, real, fun. Not corporate. Not preachy. Like telling a friend about something that actually works.
