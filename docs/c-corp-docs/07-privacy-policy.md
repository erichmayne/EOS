## 1. INTRODUCTION

RunMatch, Inc. ("RunMatch," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, website (live-eos.com), and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described herein, please do not use the Service.

## 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### 2.1 — Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly when you:

- **Create an account**: Full name, email address, phone number, password
- **Set up your profile**: Fitness level, running goals, preferred schedule
- **Make financial transactions**: Payment method information (processed by our third-party payment processor — we do not store full card numbers), deposit and withdrawal amounts
- **Set up withdrawals**: PayPal email address or Venmo handle
- **Participate in Competitions**: Competition preferences, entry contributions
- **Designate recipients**: Recipient name, email, or phone number for accountability stakes
- **Contact us**: Any information you provide in support requests or feedback

### 2.2 — Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we automatically collect:

- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage data**: Features used, pages viewed, time spent in the app, interaction patterns
- **Log data**: IP address, access times, app crashes, and performance data

### 2.3 — Fitness and Health Data

With your explicit permission, we collect fitness and health data from connected services:

- **Apple Health (HealthKit)**: Running workout data including distance, duration, pace, heart rate, GPS route data, and calories burned. We only read workout data you have authorized us to access. We do not access any health data beyond what is necessary to verify your running activities.
- **Strava**: Running activity data including distance, duration, pace, and route information, received via Strava's API and webhook integrations
- **In-app activity tracking**: Push-up counts and repetition data captured via the device camera during in-app workout sessions

**Important**: We access fitness data solely to verify completion of user-set fitness objectives and competition activities. We do not sell, share, or use fitness data for advertising purposes.

### 2.4 — Location Data

We may collect location data in the following circumstances:

- **Fitness tracking**: GPS route data from connected fitness services (Apple Health, Strava) to verify running activities
- **Competition discovery**: Approximate location to show nearby public competitions (only when you use the "Find a Competition" feature)

We do not continuously track your location in the background.

## 3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service**: Create and manage your account, process transactions, track fitness objectives, facilitate Competitions, and process accountability stake transfers
- **Verify activities**: Confirm completion of running objectives and competition activities using fitness tracking data
- **Process payments**: Facilitate deposits, withdrawals, accountability stake transfers, and Competition payouts through our third-party payment processors
- **Communicate with you**: Send account notifications, objective reminders, Competition updates, and respond to support requests
- **Improve the Service**: Analyze usage patterns to improve features, fix issues, and develop new functionality
- **Ensure fair play**: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity, data manipulation, and violations of our Terms of Service
- **Comply with legal obligations**: Respond to legal processes, enforce our Terms, and protect the rights and safety of RunMatch and our users

## 4. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 — With Other Users

- **Competition participants**: Your display name and Competition scores are visible to other participants in Competitions you join
- **Designated recipients**: Your name is shared with recipients you designate for accountability stakes
- **Leaderboards**: Your display name and performance data are visible on Competition leaderboards

### 4.2 — With Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Braintree (PayPal)** | Payment processing for deposits | Payment method details, transaction amounts |
| **PayPal** | Payout processing for withdrawals | PayPal email, payout amounts |
| **Supabase** | Database hosting | Account data, activity records (encrypted at rest) |
| **Strava** | Fitness activity verification | Strava user ID, activity data (via authorized API access) |
| **Apple (HealthKit)** | Fitness activity verification | Only data you explicitly authorize via iOS Health permissions |

### 4.3 — For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency), or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of RunMatch, our users, or the public.

### 4.4 — Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change in ownership or control of your personal information.

## 5. APPLE HEALTHKIT DATA

We comply with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:

- We **do not** use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing purposes
- We **do not** sell HealthKit data to third parties, including advertising platforms, data brokers, or information resellers
- We **do not** share HealthKit data with third parties for their marketing or advertising purposes
- We **do not** use HealthKit data for any purpose other than providing fitness tracking and accountability features within the Service
- HealthKit data is stored securely and is not commingled with advertising data
- You may revoke HealthKit access at any time through your device's Health settings

## 6. DATA SECURITY

We implement commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
- Encryption of sensitive data at rest
- Secure authentication and access controls
- Regular security assessments
- Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 7. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. We may retain certain information after account deletion for the following purposes:

- **Transaction records**: Retained for up to seven (7) years for financial reporting and tax compliance
- **Dispute resolution**: Retained as necessary to resolve disputes and enforce agreements
- **Legal obligations**: Retained as required by applicable law

Fitness activity data associated with completed objectives and Competitions is retained in anonymized or aggregated form for platform analytics.

## 8. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

### 8.1 — Account Information

You may update your account information at any time through the app's Profile settings.

### 8.2 — Data Access and Deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at connect@live-eos.com. We will respond to your request within thirty (30) days.

You may delete your account directly through the app (Profile → Account → Delete Account). Upon deletion, we will remove your personal information, subject to the retention periods described above.

### 8.3 — Fitness Data Permissions

You may revoke access to Apple Health data at any time through your device's Settings → Health → Data Access. You may disconnect your Strava account at any time through the app's Profile settings.

### 8.4 — Communications

You may opt out of non-essential communications by adjusting your notification settings in the app. Transactional communications (e.g., payment confirmations, objective deadline reminders) cannot be opted out of while your account is active.

### 8.5 — California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. As stated above, we do not sell personal information.
